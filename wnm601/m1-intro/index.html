Ziying Ye

UX Designer

Ziying Ye (b. Shanghai, China 1998) is a 3D artist living and studying in San Francisco, CA. Her practice focuses on storytelling and environment design, investigating techno-future and immateriality. She earned her Bachelor's degree in Art and Technology from the Ohio State University, and now pursuing her MA degree in Interaction & UI/UX Design at the Academy of Art University.
