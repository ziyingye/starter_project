Kung Pao Chicken

Basic Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless chicken breast cut into chunks
  • 1/3 Cucumber
  • 1/4 Carrot
  • Peanuts
  • Hot chili paste
  • Sichuan peppercorns
  • Small red peppers

Marinate Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon shaoxing wine
  • 1 tablespoon light soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon starch
  • Hot chili paste
  • Appropriate amount of salt and egg white

Sauce Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon Chinese black vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon shaoxing wine
  • 1/2 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon starch
  • Chopped green onion and garlic
